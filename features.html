Features


Project Management:

1.Create Plans and Get Payment: SaaS feature for creating and managing business plans and payments.

2.The Stunning Dashboard: Comprehensive dashboard with all essential business details.

3.Manage Staff: Easy-to-create options for managing employee and client details.

4.Assign roles to each staff: Assigning roles and permissions to staff members.

5.Employee Management: Detailed HR section for managing attendance, leaves, meetings, and more.

6.Payroll: Set and generate salaries for employees with payslip feature.

Added Performance Module: Performance tracking and goal management for employees.



Finance Management::

1.Inventory Management in Invoice and Bill: Monitor inventory status of items used in business.

2.Customer/ Vendor Statement Report: Review transaction history and details with customers/vendors.

3.Thermal Print in Sales Module: Faster thermal printing for labeling, packaging, and receipts.

4.Desktop Application for Tracking Project Hours: Track project hours with customizable settings and screenshots.

5.Email Templates: Choose from pre-defined email layouts for ready-to-use templates.

6.Added iframe embed link in the form builder: External form attachment without redirection.

Integration of Cloud Data Storage: Integration with local, AWS, and Wasabi for data storage.



Human Resource Management:

1.Manage Pre-Sale: Manage leads, deals, and estimates efficiently.

2.Added Form Builder: Create and manage various forms with different field types.

3.Added Chat Module: Direct communication with users and clients.

4.Added Contract Module: Proper documentation of client contracts.

5.Added Performance Module: Performance tracking and goal management for employees.



Customer Relations Management:

1.Slack Integration: Receive instant notifications of company activities.

2.Zoom Integration: Create and manage Zoom meetings with calendar syncing.

3.Telegram Integration: Stay connected with project activities through instant messages.

4.Twilio Integration: Receive text messages for job activities and updates.

5.Managing Items: Add, categorize, and manage items for better tracking.

6.Sales Management: Manage invoices, payments, expenses, and credit notes in one tool.

7.Reports: Detailed graphs and reports for tasks, financials, and more.



Point of Sale:

1.Budget Planner: Set and manage budgets for working capital.

2.Keep a tab on Contracts: Manage contract dates and details.

3.Events and Notice Board: Assign employees and departments to events and notices.

4.Goals and Notes: Create and display goals and add supporting files to notes.

5.Reports: Detailed reports on various aspects of the business.

6.GDPR Cookie: Simplify and save personal information for future visits.

7.Business Settings: Customize system and print settings, and manage pusher settings.
